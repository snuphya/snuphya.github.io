Hi, I am a senior undergraduate student in Physics at Seoul National University.
I am currently a research intern in the Integrated Quantum Systems Lab with Prof. Dohun Kim and working closely with Prof. Eunjong Kim for a team project.
As a passionate researcher focused on experimental quantum computation in the Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum era, I am dedicated to advancing fault-tolerant quantum computing and building novel quantum devices.
I am a prospective PhD student for the 2025 Fall semester!


Research Interest

Experimental Quantum Computation in Utility-Scale
  • Harnessing current quantum processors in utility-scale for computational tasks in Noisy Intermediate-Scale quantum era (NISQ) before fault-tolerant era.
Building New Generation Qubits for Fault-tolerant Quantum Computing
  • Designing and fabricating novel quantum devices suitable for error correction techniques which is essential for faulttolerant quantum computing.
